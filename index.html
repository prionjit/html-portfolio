Story of a Mysterious Boy



It's my pleasure to introduce myself to all of you who are interested in me. I'm Prionjit Sarker. I was born in a small village in a far far remote area from the modern world yet a beautiful place like heaven. I spent my childhood with my joint family there. I've completed my Primary Education (PSC & JSC) from Friends Kindergarten and Adarsha Shishu Niketan. I completed my Secondary Education (SSC) at Karimganj Pilot Boy's Model High School. Later from Gurudayal Government College , I completed my Intermediate Education (HSC). At this stage of my life, I got myself admitted to Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET), one of the best Engineering Universities in Bangladesh. I've completed my BSc in Engineering degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering.


Now it's time to apply my academic knowledge to the real world. I started my professional career by joining a Company named Smart Technologies BD Ltd, where I worked as a hardware support engineer. Then, I joined one of the renowned companies of Bangladesh named Walton Hi-Tech Industries Ltd. as an Electronic Control System Design Engineer. In this company I was privileged to work on different projects under the department of Refrigerator R&D. Some of the projects were: Frost Controller, Twin Cooling System Controller, Digital Display Controller design, etc.. It was a great experience for me to work at the core level. Currently, I'm working as an IT officer in the largest State Owned Commercial Bank - SONALI BANK PLC.
